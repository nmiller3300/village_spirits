Our Story

More Than a
Liquor Store

New Village Spirits has been Flowery Branch's go-to destination for fine wine, top-shelf bourbon, and premium spirits for over two decades. We are a family-owned neighborhood staple that takes pride in offering something for everyone — from the casual beer buyer to the serious collector hunting for a rare allocated bottle.

Our knowledgeable staff are passionate about what they do. Whether you need a recommendation for a dinner party or want to explore the world of craft whiskey, we are here to help you find exactly what you're looking for.
